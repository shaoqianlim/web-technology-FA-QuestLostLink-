How It Works

Three simple steps to reunite lost items with their owners

📝

1. Submit a Report

Fill in item details — title, description, category, location and contact info. Takes under 60 seconds.

🔎

2. Search & Browse

Browse the centralised database, filter by category or location, and spot matching items instantly.

✅

3. Claim & Resolve

Contact the reporter, collect your item, and mark it resolved to keep the database clean.
